## Time off and leave

### Paid time off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue **PTO** according to the accrual schedule published on the internal HR portal. PTO requests must be submitted **at least 10 business days** in advance unless your manager approves an exception for emergencies.

Unused PTO may roll over up to **5 days** into the next calendar year unless local policy states otherwise.

### Sick leave

Sick leave is for illness or medical appointments. Notify your manager **as soon as practicable** on the day you cannot work. For absences longer than **3 consecutive workdays**, HR may request a fitness-for-duty or medical certification consistent with applicable law.

### Holidays

The company observes **10 paid holidays** per calendar year. The list is posted each December for the following year.

## Conduct and escalation

We are committed to a workplace free of harassment and discrimination. Concerns should be reported through the **HR intake channel** or your people partner. **Do not use this chatbot for harassment, discrimination, or legal claims** — contact HR directly.

Formal grievance steps are described in the **Grievance Policy** document on the HR portal. This handbook summary is not a substitute for that policy.
